World Countries

How many countries are there?

The answer to the seemingly simple geographical question of "How many countries are there?" is that it depends on who's doing the counting. The United Nations, for example, recognizes 251 countries and territories. The United States, however, officially recognizes fewer than 200 nations. Ultimately, the best answer is that there are 196 countries in the world but we've listed 250 countries below.
